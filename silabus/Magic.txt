MAGIC CLASS PROGRAM SYLLABUS
CHAPTER 1: EVERYDAY OBJECT MANIPULATION
Session 1: Basic Rubber Band Magic
- Material: Introduction to magician's ethics (Magician's Code) and the importance of keeping secrets.
- Trick: Crazy Domino's Band (rubber band visually penetrates finger/thumb).
- Focus: Training fine motor coordination and understanding simple optical illusion principles.
Time Breakdown: 60 Minutes
> 10 mins: Theory, History & Ethics
> 50 mins: Practice, Drilling & Feedback
Session 2: Mentalism & Crayon Color Prediction
- Material: Introduction to the art of mind reading (Mentalism).
- Trick: Predicting the crayon color chosen by the spectator, hidden behind their back.
- Focus: Training misdirection, reading body language, and convincing communication.
Time Breakdown: 60 Minutes
> 10 mins: Theory, History & Ethics
> 50 mins: Practice, Drilling & Feedback
Session 3: Basic Number Magic (Mathematical Magic)
- Material: The art of calculation-based magic without requiring sleight of hand (Self-Working Numbers).
- Trick: Predicting the final sum before the spectator chooses numbers, or guessing the secret number they are thinking of.
- Focus: Refining presentation so mathematical tricks don't feel boring and look like real magic.
Time Breakdown: 60 Minutes
> 10 mins: Theory, History & Ethics
> 50 mins: Practice, Drilling & Feedback
Session 4: Coin Magic - Part 1 (Coin Magic Basics)
- Material: Introduction to hand anatomy and basic coin manipulation.
- Trick: Techniques for secretly hiding a coin (Classic Palm & Finger Palm) and basic tricks to make a coin vanish from the hand.
- Focus: Hand flexibility and maintaining angles so the secret remains hidden.
Time Breakdown: 60 Minutes
> 10 mins: Theory, History & Ethics
> 50 mins: Practice, Drilling & Feedback

Session 5: Coin Magic - Part 2 (Advanced Coin Magic)
- Material: Application of palming techniques to more visual advanced levels.
- Trick: Coin Through Table and manipulation techniques for a coin to mysteriously move from right to left hand.
- Focus: Timing and coordination of both hands.
Time Breakdown: 60 Minutes
> 10 mins: Theory, History & Ethics
> 50 mins: Practice, Drilling & Feedback
Session 6: Banknote Magic - Part 1 (Banknote Transformation)
- Material: Utilizing banknotes as a flexible magic medium.
- Trick: Secret folding techniques to instantly change the denomination, value, or color of a banknote in front of the spectator.
- Focus: Speed of execution (handling) and neatness of folds.
Time Breakdown: 60 Minutes
> 10 mins: Theory, History & Ethics
> 50 mins: Practice, Drilling & Feedback
Session 7: Banknote Magic - Part 2 (Pen Through Bill)
- Material: The illusion of destroying an object then restoring it to its original state.
- Trick: Penetrating a banknote (can use a borrowed bill) with a regular pen or pencil without tearing or damaging it at all.
- Focus: Spectator management and cleaning up evidence.
Time Breakdown: 60 Minutes
> 10 mins: Theory, History & Ethics
> 50 mins: Practice, Drilling & Feedback
INITIAL EVALUATION
Session 8: Mini Show
- Material: First small-scale magic performance in class.
- Trick: Each participant must perform 1-2 best tricks learned from Sessions 1 to 7 (free choice: rubber band, crayon, number, coin, or
banknote).
- Focus: Testing public performance mentality, building confidence, overcoming stage fright, and initial performance evaluation.
Time Breakdown: 60 Minutes
> 10 mins: Theory, History & Ethics
> 50 mins: Practice, Drilling & Feedback

CHAPTER 2: CLASSIC MATERIALS & SPECIAL PROPS
Session 9: Rope Magic - Part 1 (Rope Magic Basics)
- Material: Characteristics of stage magic using rope as a medium.
- Trick: Techniques for creating a secret slip knot and the illusion of a rope passing through the magician's neck or body safely.
- Focus: Fluid body movements and utilization of stage space.
Time Breakdown: 60 Minutes
> 10 mins: Theory, History & Ethics
> 50 mins: Practice, Drilling & Feedback
Session 10: Rope Magic - Part 2 (Cut and Restored Rope)
- Material: One of the most legendary rope magic tricks in the world.
- Trick: Cutting a rope into two pieces realistically with scissors, then magically restoring it whole again.
- Focus: Mastery of physical gimmicks on the rope and setting the storytelling rhythm.
Time Breakdown: 60 Minutes
> 10 mins: Theory, History & Ethics
> 50 mins: Practice, Drilling & Feedback
Session 11: Rope Magic - Part 3 (Professor's Nightmare)
- Material: A very strong macro optical illusion for close-up and stage performance.
- Trick: Three ropes of different lengths (short, medium, long) magically become equal in length when pulled.
- Focus: Closing narrative for the rope magic sequence.
Time Breakdown: 60 Minutes
> 10 mins: Theory, History & Ethics
> 50 mins: Practice, Drilling & Feedback
Session 12: Card Magic - Part 1 (Card Magic Basics)
- Material: Basics of playing card handling.
- Trick: How to hold cards correctly (Mechanic's Grip), various shuffling techniques, and tricks to find a spectator's chosen card
automatically (Self-Working Card Tricks).
- Focus: Building chemistry between hands and cards.
Time Breakdown: 60 Minutes
> 10 mins: Theory, History & Ethics
> 50 mins: Practice, Drilling & Feedback

Session 13: Card Magic - Part 2 (Card Control)
- Material: Intermediate card manipulation basics.
- Trick: Techniques to control a spectator's chosen card to the top or bottom of the deck unnoticed (Card Control), plus visual card
change tricks.
- Focus: Eliminating hand tension when performing secret sleight of hand.
Time Breakdown: 60 Minutes
> 10 mins: Theory, History & Ethics
> 50 mins: Practice, Drilling & Feedback
Session 14: Card Magic - Part 3 (Ambitious Card)
- Material: A classic card routine that is the standard for professional magicians.
- Trick: Ambitious Card Routine (a signed card always rises to the top even after being placed in the middle) and tricks to detect a
card with eyes closed.
- Focus: Assembling several short tricks into one cohesive performance.
Time Breakdown: 60 Minutes
> 10 mins: Theory, History & Ethics
> 50 mins: Practice, Drilling & Feedback
Session 15: The Secret of Thumb Tip Gimmick
- Material: Introduction to one of the most secret and versatile props in the world of magic.
- Trick: How to wear, move, and hide the Thumb Tip from the spectator's angles. Practice vanishing and reproducing a small silk or
tissue from an empty hand.
- Focus: Mastery of body language so the prop's existence is never suspected.
Time Breakdown: 60 Minutes
> 10 mins: Theory, History & Ethics
> 50 mins: Practice, Drilling & Feedback
FINAL EVALUATION
Session 16: Final Gala Show & Graduation
- Material: Full-scale final practical exam in the form of a formal performance.
- Trick: Participants design a 3-5 minute performance session combining Chapter 1 and Chapter 2 material (especially rope, cards, or
thumb tip), complete with costume and closing narrative.
- Focus: Final evaluation from the instructor, professional feedback, and graduation/appreciation session.
Time Breakdown: 60 Minutes
> 10 mins: Theory, History & Ethics
> 50 mins: Practice, Drilling & Feedback
